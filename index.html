How to Play Slice Master

Getting started with Slice Master is instant — no downloads, no sign-ups. Just one button stands between you and slicing glory. Follow these steps to master the blade:

1
Launch & Pick Your Knife

Open the game and choose from your unlocked collection of legendary knives. Each blade has its own personality — start with the classic knife and earn coins to unlock more.

2
Tap or Click to Slice

Press and hold (or tap on mobile) to swing your knife downward through objects. Release to stop. Timing your press is everything — nail the sweet spot to rack up combo multipliers.

3
Slice Everything — Avoid Bombs

Cut through fruits, food items, bricks, and everyday objects for maximum points. Watch out for bombs and protected objects — slicing those ends your run instantly.

4
Chain Combos for Huge Scores

Hit multiple objects in one swing to build combo chains. The more you connect, the higher your score multiplier climbs. Precision and timing are your best weapons.

5
Unlock Knives & Beat Levels

Earn coins with every successful slice to unlock all 10 legendary knives. Progress through increasingly challenging arcade levels and prove you're the true Slice Master.
